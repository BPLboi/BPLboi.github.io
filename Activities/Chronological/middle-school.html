Middle School Activities

Overview

Throughout middle school, I participated in my school's MathCounts, Science Olympiad, and Track/Cross Country clubs. I was quite a competitive child, so I went to a LOT of competitions related to these things. Over the summers, I enjoyed visiting my grandparents and going to SigmaCamp. In 8th grade, I joined MITP PRIMES STEP, a research group for middle schoolers, and loved it!
